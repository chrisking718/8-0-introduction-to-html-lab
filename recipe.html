The Perfect PB&J

Description

This peanuter butter and jelly sandwich is my favorite sandwich. It has the perfect balance of ingredients and looks great when made right.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • jelly or jam
  • peannut butter
  • knife or something to spread
  • Plate or Napkin

Directions

  1. Place slices of bread on plate or napkin
  2. Use the knife or spreader to spread peanut butter on one slice of the bread
  3. On the other slice of bread, spread similar amount of jelly or jam
  4. Place the two slices of bread on top of each other, with the spreaded sides in between
  5. If preferred, you may remove the crusts from your sandwich
  6. Enjoy sandwich
